ABOUT EASTCSO

(Eastern Africa Student Organization)

EASTCSO stands for the Eastern Africa Student Organization, a premier regional student body dedicated to fostering leadership, innovation, and educational growth across member institutions. Our core values of 'Achieving Excellence' guide every student to reach their full potential.

The circular logo, featuring the enduring torch of knowledge and the twin stars of achievement, represents our unwavering commitment to academic rigor and community engagement.

Our Core Objectives

  • To represent students' academic, welfare, and social interests to the administration.
  • To promote sports, talent, and cultural activities through events like the Wasomi League.
  • To foster leadership development and practical innovation among Eastern African youth.

We believe that by achieving excellence together, we build a brighter future for Africa.
